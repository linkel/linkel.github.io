About Me
updated Jan 2021

I am currently a software engineer. I work for a data visualization company, and previously worked at a fleet telematics company. Before I discovered the joy of software, I was a researcher doing analytical and formulation chemistry in the pharmaceutical industry.

I love learning more about how things work and how they came to be. The universe is vast and within every slice of a discipline we can always encounter more; at every layer of knowledge and experience there exists infinite detail.

I have been lucky to gallivant around several different fields and domains. There is always so much to learn--about small molecules and proteins, about software design, and most importantly, about people.
