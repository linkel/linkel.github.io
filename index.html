About Me
updated Mar 2020

Hi! I'm a software engineer who has most recently worked at a fleet telematics company with a mission to improve driver safety. Prior to my software development career, I was a formulation chemist in the pharmaceutical industry at a company that received FDA approval for their novel drug that treats septic shock.

I love learning more about how things work, or why they are the way they are. The universe is bizarre and within every small slice of a discipline we can always zoom in more until we reach an abyss in our understanding. From my small corner of a career journey, there's been so much to learn--about small molecules and proteins, about software design, and about people.
